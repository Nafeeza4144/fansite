About Food Fantasy

Food Fantasy is a game for IOS and Android devices. The game is available in English and Japanese. It was released on July 24'2018 by Beijing ELEX Technology Co., Ltd. Food Fantasy is a free to play game with no ads(advertisements). It is a JRPG(Japanese Role-Playing Game)/Restaurant Simulation game.

In the game, you are known as a "master attendant" and your food souls will assist and respect you. You will also get help from the characters in the game. You follow the story of someone who tries their best to help whenever they can. As you travel you will make new friends, allies and enemies. At the beginning of the game, you are helped through to get used to the different tasks. There are two main parts to the game.

In the restaurant, you have your food souls help cook the recipes and serve customers. You get gold, tips and fame. Fame can be used to expand your restaurant and level it up. Tips are bonus rewards from customers. You can use tips for food soul shards, stamina, beginner seasoning(used to level up food souls's skills) and Exorcist's gloves(gets rid of ghosts in the restaurant).

Using the food souls by your side, you will fight against fallen angels and progress through the story. As you level up, your food souls will need to level up too. Using your materials, you have to level up their special skills and they will become stronger. As you complete stages, you will get ingredients to make recipies and even feed your food souls.
